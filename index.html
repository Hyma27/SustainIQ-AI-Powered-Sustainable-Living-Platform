Energy Conservation

Tips for minimizing domestic grids, appliance configuration, and smart HVAC controls.

  • Configure programmable smart thermostats to shift temperature boundaries by 2°C.
  • Eliminate Phantom Load power drains by routing home theaters to smart power strips.
  • Convert low-efficiency halogen bulbs to high-output LED configurations.

Water Stewardship

Actionable methods for lowering urban home water withdrawals.

  • Install low-flow aerators on kitchen and vanity faucets.
  • Shorten daily shower runtimes to under 5 minutes.
  • Install greywater recycling assemblies for lawn and landscape irrigation.

Zero-Waste & Composting

Creating circular resource pathways at home.

  • Adopt a rigorous double-bin kitchen separating organic compostables.
  • Utilize smart grocery planning lists to keep food spoilage below 5%.
  • Refuse single-use plastics in grocery bags, bottles, and storage containers.
